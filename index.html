How to Play

Guess the OWL / OWCS match hidden behind the broadcast frame.

  • Enter Team 1, Team 2, the Year, and the Tournament, then lock in your guess. Every answer must be chosen from its dropdown suggestions — free text on its own won't be accepted.
  • Only 1/6 of the image shows at first. Each guess reveals another section.
  • You get 6 guesses total — the last one is made against the fully revealed image.
  • Correct fields turn green, incorrect ones turn red — reuse that information on your next guess.
  • A new puzzle unlocks every day. Once you finish today's puzzle you can't reattempt it — come back tomorrow, or replay past puzzles anytime in the Archive (archive plays don't count toward your streak).
  • Share your result once you're done!

Reveal the answer?

This ends today's attempt straight away and counts as an incomplete puzzle — you won't be able to try again until it's in the archive.
