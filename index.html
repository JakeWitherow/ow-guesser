How to Play

Guess the OWL / OWCS match hidden behind the broadcast frame.

  • Enter Team 1, Team 2, the Year, and the Tournament, then lock in your guess.
  • Only 1/6 of the image shows at first. Each guess reveals another section.
  • You get 6 guesses total — the last one is made against the fully revealed image.
  • Correct fields turn green, incorrect ones turn red — reuse that information on your next guess.
  • A new puzzle unlocks each day. Share your result once you're done!
